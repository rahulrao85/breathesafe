India has an estimated ~100 million undiagnosed obstructive sleep apnea cases. BreatheSafe identifies awareness deserts — districts where estimated OSA risk is high but public awareness is near zero — by joining 5 public datasets in BigQuery.

⚕️ Responsible AI Notice

BreatheSafe is a screening-prioritization tool, not a medical diagnostic system. Population risk scores are estimates derived from public datasets (NFHS-5, Census 2011, CPCB-style AQI, Google Trends) and do not predict individual OSA. Recommendations are intended for public health campaign planning only and should not replace clinical assessment. No individual medical advice is provided. Data sources include both real and calibrated synthetic data for demonstration purposes; for production deployment, use the complete NFHS-5 Phase 2 dataset and live Google Trends signals.
